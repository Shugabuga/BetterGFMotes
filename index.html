BetterGFMotes

What is BetterGFMotes?

BetterGFMotes is a browser plug-in that allows you to view and use additional Gravity Falls-themed emotes on Reddit.

Features

  • Adds dozens of new emotes to Reddit, and more to come!
  • See /r/gravityfalls emotes throughout Reddit
  • Search, sort, and view additional emotes
  • Adds emote modifiers, such as -r, -in, and -sepia.
  • Works natively in many popular browsers
  • Adds site-wide spoiler tags
